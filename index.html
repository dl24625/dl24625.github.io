Why Matt Bryant THE GOAT should be in the Falcons ring of honor?

He has been kicking for the falcons since 2009. Over his 9 years with the Falcons, he has played in 128 games and scored 429 points for the team. He is the franchise leading scorer. Bryant also holds the franchise record for longest field goal in franchise history. He accomplished this feat in 2009 vs the Kansas City Chiefs.

Matt Bryant is one of the most accurate kickers in Atlanta Falcon's history and his stats show that his number should be put in the ring of honor when he retires. In 2016, Bryant went to his first probowl as a Falcon and helped the team make it to their second Super Bowl. Although the Falcons lost the Super Bowl, Bryant had a spectacular year making 34 of 37 field goals and only missing 1 of his 57 extra points. He was a huge reason the team had such success in 2016.

Matt Bryant is also extremely clutch when it matters. He has won dozens of games for the Falcons on game winning field goals and never missed a playoff field goal for the Falcons. In 2012, the Falcons were down 2 points with 9 seconds leftt to the Seattle Seahawks in the NFC Divisional round when Bryant kicked a 49 yard field goal to advance the Falcons to the NFC championship game. This kick is regarded as one of the greatest moments in Atlanta Falcons history and is another sure fire reason why Bryant belongs in the Falcons ring of honor.

Bryant has given time and effort to the Atlanta community

Check out his work in the community here

He has given his time to numerous charities in the community. Every Thanksgiving he visits the Atlanta mission in Atlanta and serves Thanksgiving dinner to the women and children's shelter. Bryant has also won the Atlanta Falcons Man of the Year twice in his nine year tenure. This award is voted on by the members of the Atlanta Falcons Organization and is awarded to the player who contributes to the most to the communities of Atlanta.

Alligator Whisperer

Bryant has a secret talent of calling alligators. It just goes to show that Matt Bryant is a jack of all trades and he should be in the Falcons ring of honor. Bryant frequently calls Alligators while golfing at Arthur Blank's golf course in Hilton Head. This may seem quite irrelevant to his career in the Falcons but it just goes to show that Bryant is talented on and off the field.
